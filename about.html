ABOUT

iCare is an organization aimed at spreading awareness and campaigning against the growing soil degradation that is happening. It aims to provide a platform to ensure the safety of our land and its future. It aims to raise awareness about soil degradation and its effects on society as well as providing solutions for soil conservation. The organization has been set up to provide awareness about soil degradation and its effects on our lives. The organization was founded in 2022, by a group of people who were concerned about the growing soil degradation. They felt that something needed to be done, and so they decided to start iCare.

GOALS

Our goal here in iCare is to spread awareness of the dangers of soil degradation and get people to cooperate and contribute to our cause.
