ABOUT

iCare is a nonprofit organization dedicated to addressing the issue of soil degradation and promoting soil conservation. Founded in 2022 by a group of individuals who were concerned about the negative impacts of soil degradation on society, iCare aims to raise awareness and provide solutions to this growing problem. The organization provides a platform for individuals and communities to learn about the effects of soil degradation and to take action to protect and preserve the land. Through its campaigns and initiatives, iCare is working to ensure a safe and sustainable future for our land and the communities that depend on it.

GOALS

At iCare, our mission is to raise awareness about the dangers of soil degradation and to encourage individuals and communities to take action to protect and preserve the land. We believe that by working together, we can make a real difference in the fight against soil degradation and ensure a sustainable future for our planet. Through our campaigns and initiatives, we aim to provide the knowledge, resources, and support needed to make a positive impact on this important issue. We invite you to join us in our efforts and to contribute to our cause in any way you can. Together, we can make a difference.
